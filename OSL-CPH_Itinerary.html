Absolute Must-Sees
  • Bryggen (UNESCO) — colorful wooden wharf, walk the alleys early or late
  • Fish Market (Fisketorget) — fish soup, salmon, king crab
  • Mount Fløyen Funicular — best views, ~1.5 hrs, great at sunset
  • Bergenhus Fortress — Håkon's Hall + Rosenkrantz Tower
  • Bryggen Museum (short, great context)
  • St. Mary's Church (oldest building in Bergen)
If You Have More Time
  • Mount Ulriken Cable Car — bigger views than Fløyen
  • Nordnes Peninsula — quiet, local, coastal paths
  • KODE Museums — Edvard Munch, Norwegian design (good rainy day option)
